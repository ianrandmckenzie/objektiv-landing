NIC Interactive Media
June 2017 - Aug. 2017

North Island College's Interactive Media program is the reason Objektiv exists. After killing it in a class project with Matt Boucher and Brittany King aimed to re-design the program's website, the program hired us as interns to re-design the website for real. Led by instructor Megan Wilson, we re-designed our re-design to conform with college policies and to make it clear and understandable to an international audience.
